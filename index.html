Krevn Studio is an independent one-person studio based in Italy. Every app is conceived, designed, and built from scratch — no templates, no shortcuts.

The name carries a certain weight on purpose. Each project under this label is meant to feel like an artifact: something made with enough care that it earns a place in your life, not just a slot on your homescreen.

The studio is the umbrella. The apps are the work.
